Yep the cutoffs came out today, and I was feeling all nervous before I was checking, because I went into my portal knowing that scores came out, because my sister already checked. I checked the portal, and I got the scores I expected to get. I'm slightly relieved at this moment because I knew it would qualify for AIME. Then I just cry for like 30 min looking through some posts on aops.

Reflection:
I'll put away my complaints against possible cheaters, cuz it was not the cheaters that stopped me from getting AIME qual. 96 was a score that was simply possible if I didn't just guess last second on two problems, or got one more question right. For me, I couldn't even solve problem 5 on 12a, and if I did, the results would have been different. There's some people that goes extreme and sends them complaint emails, but I feel like that is unnecessary.

Anyways, I am proud of getting a 93. Although I mocked around 120 every time, it seems like it's confirmed my mocks isn't my actual skills. And I'm happy that I didn't get a score like AMC 12B even on AMC 12A. It is an improvement from last year's 87 on 10b, and although I spent more than like 500 hours doing this thing, I still improved quite a bit.

However, not making AIME tells me that I really need to work harder. All the time I wasted just talking to a friend about totally unnecessary things and just watching Youtube is what caused this to happen. I regret it because I know I could have tried harder, and it wasn't like "I have no regrets because I tried my best" situation. I am still going to try my best and do the next right thing, and start doing f=ma and USAPhO. I am familiar with a lot of these, so it's not really a big problem that I only got 2 months left. This time, I am going to actually try my best and have no regrets. Failing AMC hurt enough, and I am not going to let it happen again. To be honest, I should be doing USACO because I main NT and Combi, but I don't really want to be part of all these cheaters who actively cheat.

Finally, now that I think about it, I feel like it's actually better that I lost hope in AIME. My plan was to "get 14 on aime and qual for USAMO" but realistically, that isn't going to happen, and I'm not even sure if I can even qual for USAMO with my score if I had gotten 15 on it because 150 dhr is kinda....:P.
