Pftd 21


Problem:


what is the greatest prime factor of 3^7 + 6^6?\
Source: FTW(you already know)\
Genre: N\
Personal Difficulty: Early AMC 12\
Hint: factor\
Solution: Factor it into 3^6(3+2^6), and it turns into 3^6*67, and 67.... is the prime number if you didn't know, so 67 is the answer\

pftd 22


Problem:


One card drawn at a random from a standard pack of 52 cards (with the four suit-spades, hearts, diamonds, and clubs). What is the probability that the card drawn is a heart or and ace? Express your answer as a decimal t the nearest hundredth.

Source: Ftw\
Genre: C(calculation)\
Personal Difficulty: amc 10 p1\
Hint: There's total of 13 cards with different numbers\
Solution: it's obviously 16/52 but it's the computation that is hard to do in 45secs. simplify to 4/13 and the answer is 0.31
