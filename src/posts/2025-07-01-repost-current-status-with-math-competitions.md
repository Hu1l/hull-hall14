While I was studying, I was somewhat able to solve hard questions; I expected smooth sprint from q1 to q15. But q7 kinda got me for asking a question that I never learned, and I was able to solve 11-12 questions (it is 11-12 because for one I just kind of guessed; I did not solve) and I ended up getting 7 right. But here’s the problem: First of all, 7 right is horrible, considering my goal of qualifying USAMO this year, and Secondly, I actually solved all of them correctly; it was just simple miscalculations and mistakes that I had to pay enough attention to. Today I learned that I was in more terrible place then where I was really supposed to be by now, and I plan to work harder.
