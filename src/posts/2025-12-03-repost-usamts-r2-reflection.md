Ok, so now that we are done with r2, I just wanted to reflect on it.
First of all, here's my thoughts on each problem


p1: Trivial, solved it in under 15 minutes, easiest question I have seen so far.


p2: I misread the question (it seems like many people did), and probably got a zero on it. I should have questioned myself when the question was suddenly so easy, but I convinced myself that p2 is supposed to be this easy.


p3: I don't have much experience with FE's especially with functions like f(f(x)), never seen them in a problem. So I was basically new. However, there was a 50 50 shot if I get the answer or not. The problem was asking if the function exists or doesn't exist, I decided to go with it doesn't exist, and I felt like I made a fairly mathematical argument (lol). I basically limited the function form and definitely got a zero on this problem.


p4: This one was easy. Although I am not really good at geo and failed all my AMCs cuz of geo, this was really straightforward for me and fun to solve. I double checked with geogebra and desmos too lol. Hopefully my proof is good enough and will get me a double digit score on r2


p5: I couldn't solve this one. I had like 1 day to work on it, but figured I would never get it and just gave up on it. I tried doing some research on round robin tournaments and stuff and I saw some interesting stuff but nothing useful came up. None of my approaches worked and was really struggling to sleep cuz of this problem I couldn't solve


Score expectation: 51050(hopefully I hit on something on p2 during my proofs?)
