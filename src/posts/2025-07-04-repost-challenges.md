As I am aiming high for multiple things, there is always stress coming from the fact that I am not good enough, and how challenging it is. Due to this factor, I don’t want to wake up every morning; I don’t want to sleep every day; and sometimes I don’t really want to live. However, I am going through these as greater struggles will remain more memorable and enormous happiness will return when I finally accomplish my goals.

The reason I always set my goals high is because one of the quote I always keep in my mind is: “Have bigger dreams so the broken pieces are still large”, which I believe I heard it from the dad in Crayon Shin-chan. And I think it really is true when I think about it. If I consider my dream to be AIME qualification, I would only work hard enough for AIME, and I would either get AIME or not get AIME. However, if I aim for IMO gold medal, I would work a lot harder and even if I fail, I would still have an USAMO qualificaition, for math competitions reference.
